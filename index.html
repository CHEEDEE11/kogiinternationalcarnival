Welcome to the Kogi International Carnival 2024

A Message from the Governor of Kogi State

TO OUR ESTEEMED INVESTORS AND PARTICIPANTS, I am delighted to invite you to the Kogi International Carnival, a vibrant celebration of our rich cultural heritage, taking place from December 1st to 7th, 2024, in Lokoja, Kabba, Okene, and Idah. This spectacular event promises to showcase the beauty and diversity of Kogi State, providing a unique opportunity for cultural exchange, business networking, and entertainment.

Thank you for your interest in Kogi State. Sincerely, His Excellency Alhaji Ahmed Usman Ododo. Governor of Kogi State

Our Vision

As the Governor of Kogi State, my administration is committed to promoting tourism, economic growth, and cultural development. The Kogi International Carnival is a flagship event that embodies this vision, offering a platform for local and international stakeholders to converge and explore the vast potential of our state.

Why Kogi State?

Kogi State is strategically located at the confluence of the Niger and Benue rivers, offering unparalleled access to markets, natural resources, and tourism attractions. Our state is open for business, and we invite you to tap into its vast potential.

Join Us

I warmly invite you to join us at the Kogi International Carnival, where we will celebrate our diversity, foster partnerships, and unlock new opportunities for growth and development
