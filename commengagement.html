COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT AND NATIONAL UNITY

Join us at the Kogi International Carnival 2024,happening from 1st to 7th December! A week of vibrant culture,music and unforgettable celebrations awaits in kogi.Dont miss the excitement.

Community Engagement And National Unity

Perhaps the most significant aspect of the Kogi International Carnival is its power to unite. With participants from all parts of the state and beyond, the carnival will bring people together to celebrate their shared heritage. The event will transcend ethnic and religious differences, fostering a sense of pride and belonging among Kogi’s diverse communities.


Moreover, as the carnival spreads across the three senatorial districts of Kogi State and culminates in the state capital, Lokoja, it will serve as a unifying force, showcasing the spirit of cooperation and harmony that exists within the state. The festival will provide a platform for dialogue, understanding, and cooperation, as people from different backgrounds come together to celebrate their shared identity.
